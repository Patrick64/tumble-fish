Tumble Fish

You're Jim Bob the pet shop owner. Your arch nemesis, Bob Jim hid all your prize goldfish in a giant tower. You must shake them all down all 13 floors without breaking the fish bowls.

Game by Patrick Woodcock. Level design by Edward Mullins. This is an entry for the Js13kGames contest.

Mobile version (recommended) »

Tilt your mobile left/right to control. Tap the fishbowl to restart the level. Portrait mode only!

 Desktop version (still good) »

Drag level left/right with mouse. Click the button on top right of screen to restart level.
